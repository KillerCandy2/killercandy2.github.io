Nature Photography - June 2022 

My nature photography project captures various aspects of the environment. One of the photos is of a bee on cherry blossoms, a common sight in spring. The project aims to document the beauty of nature in its simplest form.

City Photography - January 2023 

My city photography project is focused on capturing different scenes in the city. One of the photos is of an alleyway with the word ‘END’ painted in large letters. This project is about documenting the various aspects of city life, from the architecture to the everyday scenes on the streets.

City Photography - July 2023

In this photo for my city photography project, I captured the towering presence of urban architecture. Looking up, the sky is framed by the silhouettes of four tall buildings, emphasizing the grandeur and scale of city structures. This image, along with others in the project, showcases the diverse facets of city life and the built environment
