I specialize in crafting minimalist, stylized profile pictures and logos as commission work. Each creation is a symphony of simplicity, where every line and curve carries profound meaning. My profile pictures distill personality into pixels, capturing essence with elegant precision

Anatomy Studies - August 2023

In order to properly understand drawing human anatomy, I investigated the structure and details of the body's structure Through careful observation, I’ve explored bones, muscles, and their connections.
